空谈不做 — Empty talk without action,
万事成难 — Everything becomes difficult.
只做不谈 — Doing without talking,
互信更难 — Makes trust hard to build.
不做不谈 — Neither doing nor talking,
难上加难 — Makes it even harder.
做做谈谈 — Doing and talking,
万事不难 — Nothing is too difficult.
